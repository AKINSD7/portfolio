AK-Tech Ola

My Teacher says, Tech isnt only coding, sure he is right,

My professional journey began in 2017 when I started as a Data Analyst. With a keen interest in deriving insights from data, I developed strong expertise in tools like Excel, Python, and Power BI. Over the years, I honed my analytical skills, helping organizations make data-driven decisions.

In 2021, I took a step further into the world of Data Science, diving deep into machine learning, predictive modeling, and data visualization. This transition allowed me to solve more complex problems and work on projects that demanded creative algorithmic thinking.

In 2023, I embarked on a new challenge: expanding my technical horizons to become a Full-Stack Engineer. I started learning web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and back-end development using frameworks and tools like Node.js and Python Flask. My goal is to combine my data expertise with full-stack development to create robust, data-driven applications.

Today, I am on a continuous learning journey, building skills to bridge the gap between data science and software development. Each step has been a rewarding challenge, and I am excited about the possibilities ahead!
